# 🖼️ Free Mockups & Templates

Pre-designed templates to showcase your work (e.g., a phone screen with your app, or a t-shirt with your design on it). Perfect for portfolios, social media, presentations, and client proposals.

---

## 1. ⭐ Mockup World

**🔗 https://www.mockupworld.co**

A curated collection of free mockups from around the web. All in one place, all free.

- **Categories:** Devices (phones, laptops, tablets), apparel, packaging, stationery
- **Best for:** Device mockups, branding presentation, portfolio pieces
- **Note:** Updated regularly with new mockups

**License:** ✅ Free for commercial & personal use. Attribution appreciated.

---

## 2. ⭐ Freepik Mockups

**🔗 https://www.freepik.com/psd/mockups**

Freepik's massive collection of PSD mockups. Thousands available.

- **Best for:** Product mockups, branding presentation, portfolio pieces
- **File format:** PSD (works with Photoshop or Photopea — the free alternative)
- **Note:** 10 free downloads/day with free account

**License:** ✅ Free tier with attribution to Freepik.

---

## 3. ⭐ Shots

**🔗 https://shots.so**

A free tool to create beautiful device mockup screenshots for social media and portfolios.

- **Best for:** Social media posts showcasing your apps/designs, portfolios
- **How to use:**
  1. Upload your screenshot
  2. Choose a device (iPhone, Android, Mac, PC)
  3. Customize background and colors
  4. Download
- **No design skills needed**

---

## 4. ⭐ Isometric Mockups

**🔗 https://www.isometricmockups.com**

Free isometric (3D-angle) device mockups.

- **Best for:** Creative portfolio pieces, modern presentations
- **Special:** Unique isometric angle — makes your work stand out

**License:** ✅ Free for commercial & personal use.

---

## 💡 Tips for Beginners

- **Mockup World** is the best place to start — everything in one place
- PSD mockups need **Photoshop** or **Photopea** (free alternative) to edit
- Use **Shots** when you need a quick, clean device mockup
- Mockups make your work look professional — great for portfolios
- Place your design in a mockup → share on social media
