1.  ⭐  MOCKUP WORLD

     🔗  https://www.mockupworld.co

     What it is:
     A curated collection of free mockups from around the web.
     All in one place, all free.

     Why use it:
     •  Curated — only the best mockups
     •  Categories: devices, apparel, packaging, stationery
     •  Updated regularly with new mockups
     •  High-quality, photorealistic

     Best for:
     Device mockups (phones, laptops, tablets), apparel, branding presentation

     License:
     ✅  Free for commercial & personal use
     ✅  Attribution appreciated


2.  ⭐  FREEPIK MOCKUPS

     🔗  https://www.freepik.com/psd/mockups

     What it is:
     Freepik's massive collection of PSD mockups. Thousands available.

     Why use it:
     •  Thousands of mockup templates
     •  PSD format (works with Photoshop or Photopea)
     •  Devices, signage, packaging, apparel
     •  10 free downloads/day with free account

     Best for:
     Product mockups, branding presentation, portfolio pieces

     License:
     ✅  Free tier with attribution to Freepik


3.  ⭐  SHOTS

     🔗  https://shots.so

     What it is:
     A free tool to create beautiful device mockup screenshots
     for social media and portfolios.

     Why use it:
     •  Create device mockups in seconds
     •  Choose from many devices (iPhone, Android, Mac, PC)
     •  Customize backgrounds and colors
     •  No design skills needed
     •  Free to use

     Best for:
     Social media posts showcasing your apps/designs, portfolios


4.  ⭐  ISOMETRIC MOCKUPS

     🔗  https://www.isometricmockups.com

     What it is:
     Free isometric (3D-angle) device mockups.

     Why use it:
     •  Unique isometric angle — stands out
     •  Professional look
     •  Free to download

     Best for:
     Creative portfolio pieces, modern presentations

     License:
     ✅  Free for commercial & personal use


💡  TIPS FOR BEGINNERS:

     •  Mockup World is the best place to start — everything in one place
     •  PSD mockups need Photoshop or Photopea (free alternative) to edit
     •  Use Shots when you need a quick, clean device mockup
     •  Mockups make your work look professional — great for portfolios
     •  Place your design in a mockup → share on social media
