1.  ⭐  COVERR

     🔗  https://coverr.co

     What it is:
     A collection of beautiful videos specifically made for website
     backgrounds and hero sections.

     Why use it:
     •  Videos designed for website backgrounds
     •  High quality, smooth footage
     •  Search by mood and category
     •  No account needed

     Best for:
     Website backgrounds, hero sections, video banners, social media clips

     License:
     ✅  Free for commercial & personal use
     ✅  No attribution required


2.  ⭐  PEXELS VIDEOS

     🔗  https://www.pexels.com/videos

     What it is:
     The video section of Pexels — same great quality as their photos.

     Why use it:
     •  1+ million videos — huge library
     •  Same quality as Pexels photos
     •  Diverse content (nature, people, business, technology)
     •  Easy search and filtering

     Best for:
     Marketing videos, social media content, website backgrounds, ads

     License:
     ✅  Free for commercial & personal use
     ✅  No attribution required


3.  ⭐  MIXKIT

     🔗  https://mixkit.co

     What it is:
     A premium-quality free video site. Also has free video templates
     for Premiere Pro and After Effects.

     Why use it:
     •  Very high quality — looks like paid stock footage
     •  Free video templates (Premiere Pro, After Effects, Final Cut)
     •  Motion graphics included
     •  Also has free music and sound effects

     Best for:
     Professional videos, video templates, motion graphics, intros

     License:
     ✅  Free for commercial use
     ✅  No attribution required


4.  ⭐  PIXABAY VIDEOS

     easy-to-use license.

     Why use it:
     •  Good variety, free for anything
     •  No account needed
     •  Same simple license as Pixabay photos

     Best for:
     Quick finds, variety, supplementary footage

     License:
     ✅  Free for commercial use
     ✅  No attribution required

6.  ⭐  VIDEVO

     🔗  https://www.videvo.net

     What it is:
     A professional stock video website with both free and premium clips.

     Why use it:
     •  Professional quality footage
     •  Large library of free clips
     •  Free plan available

     Best for:
     Professional video projects, commercial use

     License:
     ✅  Free tier with attribution to Videvo


💡  TIPS FOR BEGINNERS:

     •  Start with Coverr or Pexels Videos — easiest to use
     •  Use Mixkit if you need professional-looking footage
     •  Most videos come in MP4 format (works everywhere)
     •  Check the resolution before downloading (1080p is standard)
     •  For website backgrounds, choose loop-friendly videos
