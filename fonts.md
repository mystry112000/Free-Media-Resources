1.  ⭐  GOOGLE FONTS

     🔗  https://fonts.google.com

     What it is:
     The biggest and most popular free font library. Made by Google.
     Every font is free for commercial use.

     Why use it:
     •  1,800+ font families — huge variety
     •  All fonts are open source and free
     •  Easy to use on websites (just copy-paste code)
     •  Variable fonts, icons, and AI-powered font pairings
     •  Test fonts before downloading

     Best for:
     Web design, print, branding, logos, app design — everything

     License:
     ✅  Free for commercial use
     ✅  Open source — modify and share
     ✅  No attribution required


2.  ⭐  FONT SQUIRREL

     🔗  https://www.fontsquirrel.com

     What it is:
     A carefully curated collection of free fonts. Every font here is
     hand-checked to be 100% free for commercial use.

     Why use it:
     •  All fonts cleared for commercial use
     •  Curated — quality over quantity
     •  Web font generator included (convert fonts for web use)
     •  No account needed

     Best for:
     Commercial projects, professional design, web fonts

     License:
     ✅  Free for commercial use
     ✅  No attribution required


3.  ⭐  DAFONT

     🔗  https://www.dafont.com

     What it is:
     A massive community-driven font library. Huge variety of styles
     — especially decorative and display fonts.

     Why use it:
     •  Huge collection — thousands of fonts
     •  Great for decorative, display, and themed fonts
     •  Categorized by style (gothic, comic, sci-fi, etc.)
     •  Preview text before downloading

     Best for:
     Decorative fonts, themed designs, personal projects, posters

     License:
     ✅  Free for personal use
     ⚠️  Check each font for commercial license — some require purchase


4.  ⭐  FONTSPACE

     🔗  https://www.fontspace.com

     What it is:
     A community platform where indie font designers share their work.

     Why use it:
     •  100,000+ fonts — very large library
     •  Unique, indie fonts you won't find elsewhere
     •  Direct from font designers
     •  Preview and test before downloading

     Best for:
     Unique fonts, indie designs, creative projects

     License:
     ✅  Free for personal use
     ⚠️  Check each font for commercial use


💡  TIPS FOR BEGINNERS:

     •  Start with Google Fonts — safest choice, free for everything
     •  Use Font Squirrel when you need commercial-safe fonts
     •  Use DaFont when you need fun, decorative, or themed fonts
     •  Font files come in different formats:
        - TTF/OTF: Works on your computer (install like software)
        - WOFF/WOFF2: For websites
     •  Don't use too many fonts in one design — 2-3 fonts maximum
     •  Pair fonts carefully: one for headings, one for body text
