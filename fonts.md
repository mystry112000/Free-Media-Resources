# 🖊️ Free Fonts & Typography

Different styles of text/lettering. Perfect for websites, logos, posters, social media, and print materials.

---

## 1. ⭐ Google Fonts

**🔗 https://fonts.google.com**

The biggest and most popular free font library. Made by Google. Every font is free for commercial use.

- **Library:** 1,800+ font families
- **Best for:** Web design, print, branding, logos, app design — everything
- **Special:** Variable fonts, icons, and AI-powered font pairings

**License:** ✅ Free for commercial use. Open source. No attribution needed.

---

## 2. ⭐ Font Squirrel

**🔗 https://www.fontsquirrel.com**

Carefully curated free fonts. Every font here is hand-checked to be 100% free for commercial use.

- **Best for:** Commercial projects, professional design, web fonts
- **Also has:** Web font generator (convert fonts for web use)

**License:** ✅ Free for commercial use. No attribution needed.

---

## 3. ⭐ DaFont

**🔗 https://www.dafont.com**

Massive community-driven font library. Huge variety — especially decorative and display fonts.

- **Best for:** Decorative fonts, themed designs, personal projects, posters
- **Categorized by:** Gothic, comic, sci-fi, calligraphy, etc.

**License:** ✅ Free for personal use. ⚠️ Check each font for commercial license.

---

## 4. ⭐ Fontspace

**🔗 https://www.fontspace.com**

Community platform where indie font designers share their work.

- **Library:** 100,000+ fonts
- **Best for:** Unique fonts, indie designs, creative projects

**License:** ✅ Free for personal use. ⚠️ Check each font for commercial use.

---

## 💡 Tips for Beginners

- Start with **Google Fonts** — safest choice, free for everything
- Use **Font Squirrel** when you need commercial-safe fonts
- Use **DaFont** when you need fun, decorative, or themed fonts
- **Font file formats:**
  - **TTF/OTF:** Works on your computer (install like software)
  - **WOFF/WOFF2:** For websites
- Don't use too many fonts in one design — 2-3 fonts maximum
- Pair fonts carefully: one for headings, one for body text
